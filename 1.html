FAQs

1. How do I place an order?

You can place an order by browsing our products, adding your desired items to the cart, and completing the checkout process using your preferred payment method.

2. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major debit/credit cards, UPI, net banking, digital wallets (like Paytm, PhonePe), and Cash on Delivery (COD).

3. How long does delivery take?

Delivery usually takes 2-5 business days, depending on your location. We'll provide tracking details once your order is shipped.

4. Can I track my order?

Yes! Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive an email or SMS with the tracking link so you can follow your package in real-time.

5. What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day easy return policy on most items. If you’re not satisfied with your purchase, you can request a return or exchange.

6. Is it safe to shop on your site?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and secure payment gateways to ensure your data is protected.

7. Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship within India. We plan to expand internationally soon—stay tuned!

8. Are there any shipping charges?

Shipping is free on orders above ₹499. Orders below this amount may include a small delivery fee, shown at checkout.

9. Can I cancel my order after placing it?

Yes, you can cancel your order within 2 hours of placing it from your account dashboard or by contacting our support team.

10. How do I contact customer support?

You can reach us at support@shopmart.com or call us at +91-00009999000 between 10 AM – 6 PM, Monday to Saturday.
